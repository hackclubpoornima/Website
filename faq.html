Questions Answers
What is Hack Club Poornima? Hack Club Poornima is a club within the hack club umbrella
What is the mission of Hack Club? To teach CS/SE through flexible non-cumulative workshops and provide opportunities of collaborative work on projects by the Hack Club Poornima Students including software, hardware, art, and events(hackathons, hackjams, etc).
What is Hack Club? Hack Club is an American based nonprofit organization helping establish Coding Clubs.
Where can I find the Hack Club Poornima On this site, and nearly on all the social media.
How can students join? All students can join our workshops, at any time in the year through registering in the events.
What can students expect? An hour-long workshop where they get to learn how to build something with Computer Science/Software Engineering with great people and the opportunity to work on club projects, events, and management. Also, we may have snacks, stickers, and other freebies.
What kind of experience do students need? Students don’t need any to join our workshops, but any knowledge of technical skills can play into the creation of their work.
What kind of things will workshops be about? Web Development, Python, Discord Bot development, Machine Learning, Game Development, etc. You can also provide with your own topic and we'll work on it.
How can you help our Hack Club create an amazing depth of opportunity for all kinds of students? 1. Join our workshops and spread the news. If you’re a teacher, faculty, student, etc.
2. Tell us your woes, we love to solve problems and we love solving problems that affect us.
3. Connect with us, we are always looking for sponsors, partners, and making relationships with other clubs.
4. Ask your friends to join, as long as they think they can create things, they are welcome.
