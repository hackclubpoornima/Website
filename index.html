Hack Club Poornima

A nonprofit network of computer hackers, makers, and coders.

Why is it called Hack Club?

The term "Hacking" does not relate to hacking bank accounts or computer systems, but rather it is being creative and making something awesome. "Hack Club" conducts hackathons, events, and workshops in which students use technology to build whatever they desire. We are a hack club because we share the same spirit of making.
